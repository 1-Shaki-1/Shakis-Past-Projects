Arachnobots FTC Team Home Page

ARACHNOBOTS is a TDSB-affiliated high school robotics team from West Hill Collegiate Institute, Scarborough. Founded in the 2024-2025 school year. We have more than ten students showing passion, who aim to be future engineers, coders and designers. All of them share interest in science, technology, engineering, math next to robotics.

We Inspire To...

  • Gain hands-on experience
  • Learn Problem-Solving Skills
  • Foster Teamwork and Collaboration

    • What is FTC?

    FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an international robotics competition for students aged 12 to 18 where teams design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in a head-to-head challenge. Guided by adult mentors, students develop STEM skills, teamwork, and engineering principles. Beyond the technical aspects, teams also manage their brand, raise funds, and engage in community outreach.

    Our Projects

    Robot Designs Over The Years

    Charlotte V1:

    Our first robot, Charlotte V1, was built for the 2025-2026 FTC season. It featured a mecanum wheel drivetrain for omnidirectional movement, a flywheel shooter for launching artefacts. There was little to no autonomous functionality implemented due to time constraints and delayed part deliveries.

    Goals

    What we aim to develop

    • Technical Proficiency: Gaining hands-on experience with engineering principles, computer-aided design (CAD), and programming (including autonomous control and vision), while learning to work effectively with tools and equipment.
    • Teamwork and Collaboration: Fostering communication, leadership, and teamwork by collaborating closely with members, mentors, and other teams.
    • Innovation and Problem-Solving: Encouraging creative thinking, rapid prototyping, and adaptability when facing design and competition challenges.
    • Community and Outreach: Promoting STEM education by engaging with local students, schools, and community programs to inspire future innovators.
